### Introduction

The objective of this page is to provide keys for motor Rapid Control Prototyping. The RCP chosen system is [MPLAB Device Blocks for Simulink](https://www.microchip.com/DevelopmentTools/ProductDetails/sw007023#utm_source=MicroSolutions&utm_medium=Link&utm_term=FY16Q4&utm_content=DevTools&utm_campaign=Article). It allows the use of Matlab Simulink the directly target Microchip Microcontroller. Originally intended to  5th year students in the [electrical engineering department](http://ge.insa-lyon.fr/) of [INSA Lyon](http://www.insa-lyon.fr/) for the [CCSA](http://ge-option5a.insa-lyon.fr/content/ccsa-commande-de-convertisseurs-et-de-systemes-dactionnement) option, the objective of these pages is to propose an initiation to the programming environment toolbox [MPLAB Device Blocks for Simulink](https://www.microchip.com/DevelopmentTools/ProductDetails/sw007023#utm_source=MicroSolutions&utm_medium=Link&utm_term=FY16Q4&utm_content=DevTools&utm_campaign=Article).
